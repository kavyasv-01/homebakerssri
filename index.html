Jayasri's Journey

What started as a passionate love affair with flavors in the heart of a home kitchen blossomed beautifully into Homebakershyd. Jayasri turned a simple dream into a premium local brand dedicated to baking perfection.

Every recipe has been tested, tweaked, and perfected over countless hours to ensure it strikes the absolute finest balance of moisture, flavor, and texture. This isn’t commercial baking—it’s home craft at its highest tier.
